I am currently a senior algorithm expert at the Security Department of Alibaba Group.
I got my Ph.D. degree from the Department of CIS, University of Macau in 2016. Prior that, I received my bachelor degree in software engineering from Xidian University, Xi'an. My research focuses on spatio-temporal data management and minng, machine learning and high performance parallel computing.
