Short Bio

Yuhong Li is currently a senior algorithm engineer in the Security Department of Alibaba Group.
He got his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer and Information Science, University of Macau, under the supervision of Prof. Leong Hou U and Prof. Zhiguo Gong in 2016. His research focuses on spatio-temporal database, data mining for social security and high performance parallel computing.
