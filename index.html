Yuhong Li is currently a senior algorithm engineer at the Security Department of Alibaba Group.
He got his Ph.D. degree from the Department of CIS, University of Macau in 2016. His research focuses on spatio-temporal data management and minng, machine learning and high performance parallel computing.
